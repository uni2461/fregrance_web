ABOUT US

A one-of-a-kind fragrance,

■Brand message

Fragrance, made more freely.

There are no fixed rules for fragrance.
You can choose a scent that matches your mood, or create one while thinking of someone special. Fragrance should be more playful, more personal, and more creative. We value the joy of choosing and the experience of changing how you feel through scent.

■Three Key Features

1. Freely choose from 30 fragrance notes

2. Co-create with a professional perfumer

3. Name your fragrance and complete the label on the spot

■ A Special Bottle, Created in a Small Atelier


Our cozy atelier is a calm space where you can take your time choosing scents. Perfect as a travel memory or a refresh for everyday life. Together, we shape a fragrance that feels uniquely you.

Q&A

Common Question

  • Q1.How long does the experience take?

    • A.About 30 minutes.。
      Consultation → Scent tasting → Blending → Bottling → Completion.

  • Q2. How long does the fragrance last?

    • A.Approximately 3-4 hours.
      Longevity may vary depending on body temperature and environment.

  • Q3.Can I reorder the same fragrance later?

    • A.Yes, you can.
      We keep your fragrance recipe,
      so you can reorder the same scent at any time.

  • Q4. I have scents I don’t like. Is that okay?

    • A.Absolutely.
      You will choose only the scents you like during the tasting session.
      We offer a wide variety of fragrance notes so you can find something that suits you.

  • Q5.Can I make it as a gift?

    • A.Yes.Gift wrapping is available.

  • Q6.Is English support available?

    • A.Yes.Our staff can assist you in English, and in-store instructions are available in English.

  • Q7.What if I don’t like the scent?

    • A.Our perfumer will work with you to adjust the fragrance until it matches your ideal scent.
